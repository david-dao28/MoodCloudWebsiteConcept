Hi, welcome to MoodCloud.

MoodCloud's mission is to help our users understand how their moods, habits, and environments impact

their well-being. We envision a world of more health-conscious, productive, and interconnected people.

MoodCloud enables people to take control of their lives through a gamified, personalized, and

user-friendly platform.

App Features

  • Mood tracking

  • In-depth data analytics

  • Machine learning algorithms

  • Customizable, digital pet that reflects how you're feeling
